About Me

My goal is to extend the capabilities of biomedicine by applying and developing new machine learning tools. Having graduated with a BSc. in Electrical Engineering from The Technion, I have decided to take my knowledge and apply it in a practical way to help scientists and doctors. Biomedical research has drawn my attention not only because it is fascinating but also because it is highly important given the challenges we face in recent years. I believe that my experience in machine learning and signal analysis will be valuable for biomedical research due to the growing impact of automated solutions on various challenges.
